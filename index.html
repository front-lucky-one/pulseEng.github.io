Pulseteam

is a service for quick, convenient and regular feedback collection in companies.

We have created a tool that helps companies to achieve the following goals:

—set up a regular exchange of ideas in a team or company

—automate routine HR processes

—collect feedback from customers, partners and contractors

Be the first one to try it out!

Why is this?

When my company staff increased to 50+ people, I realised I no longer understood each employee’s motivation, problems, achievements and desires. Without this data the quality of the decisions I had to make as a CEO was falling. It led to problems in production and personnel processes and demotivated my employees.

I tried to use conventional tools to collect regular feedback (e-mails, Google forms, chats, channels, task trackers, meetings), and encountered quite a few problems:

—Using all of these tools for regular data collection is really time-consuming
— Data formats differ; there is no common data storage and analysis system

What is Pulseteam??

Pulseteam is a survey building tool with extensive automation possibilities, convenient mobile application for employees and analytics system for a handy and secure access to the data.

SAMPLE SETTINGS

Anonymous survey of manager's work

Every two weeks all employees on the project team take a survey about the quality of management on the project. Results are sent to the head of Management Department.

Interview

All employees who take part in interviewing potential candidates are sent a form in which they can describe their impressions.

Workflow monitoring

Daily pulse survey for developers is a short form with a few questions: — What did you manage to get done yesterday? — What are you going to do today? — Are there any problems? Results are delivered to the manager as a report.

Probation

When a new employee is added to the system, the probationary algorithm is started: on appointed dates, the employees responsible for the new recruit fill in the form to assess his or her success. The results are delivered to the HR Manager.

Customer feedback request

Once a week managers working on the project on the part of a VIP customer are sent forms to give feedback on the project team. The report with the results is delivered to the Account Director.

Congratulations and parting words

The day before an employee's birthday all other team members get a form in which they leave congratulations for the birthday boy. On the date of the birthday, the celebrant receives a report with all the congratulations submitted.

After-meeting surveys

After each meeting all its participants receive a form asking to assess the meeting and comment on its results. An employee responsible for organizing a meeting receives a form which can be used to give a meeting report.

Polls

Company managers hold polls among employees through Pulseteam (e.g., where to hold a corporate event, on what dates to arrange a workshop, what courses to choose for staff development programs).

Why is it so convenient?

There is a lot of interesting and useful settings integrated in Pulseteam:

Google Suite Integration

Create your company and add employees in one click.

Order in the team

Assign employees to groups, departments, divisions and branches to manage your targeting in seconds.

Template design

Create a survey, set it as a template, and - starting now - Pulseteam will regularly send it out to the right respondents.

Calendar integration

Want to collect data about certain events? Bind a template with your calendar, and Pulseteam will do the rest.

Workspaces

If you have multiple workspaces, switch between companies in one click.

Personal feedback

Give employees the opportunity to publicly or privately give feedback to each other.

Request feedback

Each user can request feedback about themselves from any member of the team.

Process design
